Interests

I have a variety of interests that keep me inspired and motivated:

Jazz Musician

I am a passionate jazz drummer who brings rhythm and energy to various venues around town, performing for both businesses and private events. The image on the left features my favorite drummer, Max Roach, whose innovative techniques and musicality have deeply inspired my playing style. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a lively corporate event, I strive to create an unforgettable musical experience.

Puzzle Games

I have a deep passion for puzzle games, enjoying the intellectual challenge they provide. From solving the Rubik's Cube to navigating escape rooms, and tackling Sudoku, I thrive on the mental stimulation they offer. One of my favorite challenges is the sliding puzzle, where you rearrange scrambled pieces in a box with one empty spot to form the correct order. These games are not just a pastime for me; they are a way to sharpen my problem-solving skills and keep my mind agile.
